Food

The diverse and ever-evolving gastronomic scene of Greece will surprise all visitors pleasantly , allowing you to have unforgettable culinary experiences, no matter what your preferences are. The traditional Mediterranean cuisine is amazing of course, but those who enjoy more sophisticated tastes will not be disappointed either. From fast food to fine dining and international cuisine, including mostly Asian or European restaurants, Greece offers a wide variety of options to try. Plant-based-friendly restaurants, traditional taverns, charming cafes and cozy bars can be found in Greece. Depending on your location, the available options may vary. For instance, mountainous destinations offer mostly traditional cuisine and touristy locations have the most diverse dining facilities. In our Eat & Drink section you will discover some of the best places for your lunch, dinner, breakfast, cocktail or dessert.
